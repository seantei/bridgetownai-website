Why Choose On-Premise AI Over Cloud AI?

On-premise AI (also called local AI, private AI, or self-hosted AI) keeps your business data on hardware you own and control. Cloud AI services like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot process your data on shared servers. For businesses handling sensitive information ‚Äî client data, financial records, trade secrets, legal documents ‚Äî on-premise AI provides superior privacy, security, and compliance with data protection regulations.
